Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Application

With the rise of technology, remote healthcare solutions are more critical than ever. Our Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App leverages Flask and a Decision Tree Classifier model to help users identify potential illnesses based on their reported symptoms.

The Problem

Users often experience symptoms and want quick answers about their health. Our application helps differentiate between overlapping symptoms to provide a preliminary diagnosis.

The Solution

  1. Symptom Input: Users enter their symptoms via an intuitive interface.
  2. Machine Learning Model: A Decision Tree Classifier predicts potential diseases.
  3. Prediction: The app provides real-time disease predictions.
  4. Additional Information: Disease descriptions, precautions, medications, and more.

Key Features

  • Intuitive Interface: Easy navigation for users.
  • Accurate Predictions: AI-driven analysis for health concerns.
  • Comprehensive Information: Diet, medications, and workout advice included.

Conclusion

By combining machine learning with healthcare, we aim to make health insights more accessible. This tool serves as an educational resource and a preliminary step for users seeking medical advice.
